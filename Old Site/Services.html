Retirement-focused Transitions

My interest in midlife transitions and healthy aging developed during my time at Harvard University Health Services, where I provided therapy to faculty and staff. When Harvard introduced a voluntary early retirement program in 2020, I saw how many individuals approaching this life stage were seeking more guidance and meaningful support. Drawing from my background in geriatric psychiatry and informed by current research, I designed and facilitated a six-week retirement support group to help individuals navigate this pivotal life shift.

I now offer personalized coaching or counseling for those preparing for or adjusting to retirement. These one-on-one sessions provide space to reflect on your experiences, clarify your values, and explore what this next chapter can look like for you—with purpose, confidence, and intention. This retirement-focused service is available as either counseling or coaching, depending on your goals and needs.
‍
Six 50-minute sessions: $1,500
Each additional session: $250
